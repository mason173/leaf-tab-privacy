Privacy Policy

This extension respects your privacy and does not collect personal data.

Data stored by the extension is limited to user preferences, shortcuts, and settings required for the new tab functionality. This information is stored locally or synced through the user's browser account.

Weather information and daily wallpapers are fetched from external services to provide updated content, but no personal information is transmitted.

The extension does not sell, track, or share user data with third parties.
