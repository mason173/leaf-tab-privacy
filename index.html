LeafTab Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

LeafTab ("we", "us", or "our") takes your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our browser extension.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We follow the principle of data minimization. We only collect the data necessary to provide the extension's functionality.

  • Local Data: Your shortcuts, background settings, and preferences are stored locally in your browser by default (using localStorage or chrome.storage).
  • Cloud Sync (Optional): If you choose to log in, your shortcut groups will be encrypted and synchronized to our server (leaftab.cc) to allow you to access your data across multiple devices. We will not sell or share this data with any third parties.

2. Permissions Explained

LeafTab requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Geolocation: Used solely to display local weather information. Your location information is only used to query for weather and is not saved on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide a better experience, we may interact with the following third-party services:

  • Weather Providers: Open-Meteo, BigDataCloud, or Nominatim (for fetching weather data).
  • Favicon Providers: Google Favicon API, DuckDuckGo, etc. (for retrieving website icons).

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data. All communication between the extension and the sync server is encrypted using HTTPS.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the following method:

2. 权限说明

LeafTab 需要以下权限才能正常工作：

  • 地理位置 (Geolocation)： 仅用于显示当地天气信息。您的位置信息仅用于查询天气，不会被保存到我们的服务器。

为了提供更好的体验，我们可能会与以下第三方服务交互：
